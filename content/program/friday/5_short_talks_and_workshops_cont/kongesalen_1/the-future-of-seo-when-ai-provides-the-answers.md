As more searches end without a click and AI delivers answers directly across search results and AI chat, the rules of SEO are changing. It’s no longer just about ranking first, but about becoming a source AI uses in its answers.

In this talk, you’ll learn how to optimize websites and content for both humans and machines, and how to approach SEO in a world where the user journey often starts (and sometimes ends) in the search results.
