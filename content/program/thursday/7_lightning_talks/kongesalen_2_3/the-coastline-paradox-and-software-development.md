How long is a country's coastline? It turns out that the more precise you try to measure it, the longer it becomes. But wait a minute, isn't that just like trying to estimate a software development project? Yes, it is, and actually there's a good reason for that too. Join this lightning talk to find out why, and what consequences this has for your next software project!
