Creating a thriving professional community is essential for every workplace. It can be particularly challenging for groups outside a company's core business areas.
I am a software developer passionate about community building who works at an energy company. 
In this talk, I will reflect on what makes a workplace feel welcoming and inclusive. We will dive into good practices for establishing a dev community within a non-dev company.
