Norway's largest newspaper vg.no successfully migrated from physical data centers to Varnish Cache on AWS, implementing large-scale clusters in Kubernetes to serve millions of requests per second. Get to know more about how the  migration delivered improved performance through optimized caching strategies and auto-scaling, while significantly reducing infrastructure costs and enhancing operational flexibility.
