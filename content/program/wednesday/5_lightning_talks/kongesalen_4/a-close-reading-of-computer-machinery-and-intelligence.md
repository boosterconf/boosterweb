In the age of the LLM, the Turing Test is more relevant than ever. But what *is* the Turing Test? How did Turing envision the Imitation Game, as he called it, and what does he see as its greatest limitations? 

In this session, Felienne will take you on wild ride through a seminal paper in computer science, philosophy and computation.
