Is experiencing a high cognitive load always bad? Does it make sense to talk about cognitive load on the team level? Are we better able to think about the big problems if we offload the small ones?

Cognitive Load Theory has risen in popularity the last few years, and rightly so! Developers and architects have much to learn from psychology. But with popularity comes misunderstandings, since few ever take the time to read the actual research. To rid us of the misunderstandings I will take you through the concepts you can use to develop a clearer vocabulary when talking about cognitive load.
