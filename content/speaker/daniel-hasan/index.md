Daniel is a UX designer working for Netlife Design in Oslo, Norway. He has worked with national and international customers such as Telenor, Norwegian, Gjensidige, the Norwegian Labour and Welfare Administration and the Norwegian Digitalisation Agency, helping people with everything from buying their plane tickets to getting child benefit from the government.

Daniel is passionate about diversity and sustainability in design, and strongly believes that the people who create digital products and services need to work harder to make sure that what we make is good both for the people using them, and for the world.
