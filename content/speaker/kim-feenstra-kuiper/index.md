Kim Feenstra Kuiper is a seasoned UX researcher with experience at companies like Spotify, Goodnotes, and King (makers of Candy Crush). Through her consultancy, Expedition UX, she helps product teams go beyond usability testing—using deep, practical research methods to understand real user behavior. Kim is passionate about making UX research accessible for cross-functional teams and embedding it in fast-paced product development to create products people truly want to use.
